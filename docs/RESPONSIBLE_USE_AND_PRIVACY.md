## üéì Academic Integrity Policy

**Our AI tools are study aids, not shortcuts.**

### Responsible Use of AI Essay Assistance

**‚úÖ Allowed** (encouraged!):
- Brainstorming essay topics and angles
- Getting feedback on grammar and clarity
- Identifying weak arguments or gaps in logic
- Learning to structure compelling narratives
- Editing for tone and style

**‚ùå Not Allowed** (academic dishonesty):
- Submitting AI-generated essays as your own work
- Copying AI suggestions verbatim without attribution
- Using AI to write entire essays from scratch
- Bypassing essay prompts with generic responses
- Fabricating experiences or achievements

### Our Detection & Enforcement

**We Monitor**:
- Unusual essay similarity patterns
- Excessive AI reliance (>80% AI-generated content)
- Copy-paste from AI without editing
- Generic responses (not personalized to you)

**Consequences**:
1. **First Violation**: Warning + education
2. **Second Violation**: Temporary account suspension
3. **Third Violation**: Permanent ban + notify scholarship providers

### Your Responsibility

**Before Submitting**:
- ‚úÖ Read and understand every sentence
- ‚úÖ Personalize AI suggestions to your voice
- ‚úÖ Fact-check all claims (AI can hallucinate)
- ‚úÖ Ensure honesty (don't exaggerate achievements)
- ‚úÖ Follow scholarship provider guidelines (some ban AI use)

**Golden Rule**: If you can't explain it in an interview, don't put it in your essay.
